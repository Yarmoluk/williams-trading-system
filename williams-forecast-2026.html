03 / 08

Decennial Pattern (Years Ending in 6)

Based on Edgar Lawrence Smith's work from the 1930s, years ending in 6 show a distinctive pattern:

  • Strong preponderance of rising markets from July to November
  • Weakness starts in May, important low in late July
  • Confirms the 4-year cycle forecast
Decennial pattern and 4-year cycle both point to: summer low → strong second-half rally.

Presidential Cycle

2026 = 2nd year of presidential term. The historical pattern shows:

  • March peak
  • Decline into summer
  • Massive rally from July onward
"The pattern is apparent regardless of who is partying in the White House."
Marc Chaikin predicted a bear market starting March 16, 2026. Williams disagrees on the severity but agrees on March weakness. Today is that date.

Natural Cycle

For 2026, the Natural Cycle shows: almost straight-up continuation of the bull market.

CONFLICT

RESOLUTION

Recession

No recession coming. The recession cycle says don't worry until mid-2027. FRED Smoothed Recession Probabilities = near zero.

Employment

Employment cycle low = end of Q1 2026, then improving — historically bullish for stocks.

Fed Funds

Rates declining — "every time the discount rate dropped, stocks rallied."

Valuation

DJIA P/E at 23.15 (forward) — better than last year's 27. Suggests ~7.5% annual return. P/E > 28 = cause for concern. Watch earnings.
